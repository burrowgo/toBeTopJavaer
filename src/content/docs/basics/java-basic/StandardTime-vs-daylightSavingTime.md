---
title: "Standardtime Vs Daylightsavingtime"
---

Daylight Saving Time (DST) and Standard Time exist to make full use of daylight during the summer; therefore, clocks are set forward one hour in the summer and set back one hour in the winter. Daylight Saving Time typically lasts from the second Sunday in March to the first Sunday in November.

Standard Time (Winter Time):
Time difference between Beijing and Los Angeles: 16 hours
Time difference between Beijing and New York: 13 hours

Daylight Saving Time (Summer Time):
Time difference between Beijing and Los Angeles: 15 hours
Time difference between Beijing and New York: 12 hours
